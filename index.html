Trezor Suite App (Official) | Desktop & Web Crypto Management

The Trezor Suite is a powerful desktop and web application for managing your crypto assets. Designed for ease of use and robust security, it offers a user-friendly interface to access, send, and receive cryptocurrency. Key features include:

  • Secure Management: Safely manage multiple cryptocurrencies in one place.
  • Real-Time Portfolio Tracking: Monitor asset values with real-time updates.
  • Advanced Tools: Features like coin control and transaction labeling enhance usability.
  • Cross-Platform Accessibility: Available on Windows, macOS, Linux, and as a browser extension.

Setting up Trezor Suite is effortless. Simply download it from the official website, connect your Trezor device, and follow the prompts. With Trezor Suite, security meets simplicity.

Trezor Bridge: The Connectivity Gateway

The Trezor Bridge acts as the link between your Trezor device and your computer. This lightweight application ensures smooth communication between the hardware wallet and your browser. With Trezor Bridge, you can:

  • Access the Trezor Suite directly through your preferred web browser.
  • Enjoy seamless compatibility with various operating systems.
  • Stay updated with regular improvements in version v24.11.3.

Setting up the Trezor Bridge is quick and easy. Head to Trézor.io/Start and download the latest version to unlock its full potential.
